about us

Better Grade Tracker is a way for students to keep track of how they're doing in classes without the extra hassle of creating their own Excel spreadsheets or re-calculating each time they want to know their grade. Simply create an account, add your classes and their grading schemes, and insert your grades, and you'll be able to see your current projected grade. Happy studying! :)
